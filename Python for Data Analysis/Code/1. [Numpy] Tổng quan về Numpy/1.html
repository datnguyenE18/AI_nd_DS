3. Thao tác với mảng¶

  • dtype: Kiu d liu ca phn t trong mng.
  • shape: Kch thc ca mng.
  • size: S phn t trong mng.
  • ndim: S chiu ca mng.
In [12]:

arr.dtype

dtype('float64')

Out[13]:

arr_1.size
Out[14]:

In [15]:
# S chiu ca mng:
arr_1.ndim
Out[15]:

Truy cp phn t trong mng:

Cc phn t trong mng c nh s t 0 tr i

  • arr[ i ]: Truy cp ti phn t th i ca mng 1 chiu.
  • arr1[ i , j ]: Truy cp ti phn t hng i, ct j ca mng 2 chiu.
  • arr2[ n, i, j ]: Truy cp ti phn t chiu n, hng i, ct j ca mng 3 chiu.
  • arr[ a : b ]: Truy cp ti cc phn t t a n b - 1 trong mng 1 chiu.
  • arr1[ : , :i ]: Truy cp ti phn t t ct 0 n ct i - 1, ca tt c cc hng trong mng 2 chiu.
In [16]:
print("arr3[2,1,3] =", arr3[2,1,3])

arr3[2,1,3] = 8

Cc hm thng k:

  • arr.max() hoc np.max(arr): Ly gi tr ln nht ca mng arr.
  • arr.min() hoc np.min(arr): Ly gi tr nh nht ca mng arr.
  • arr.sum() hoc np.sum(arr): Tng tt c cc phn t trong mng arr.
  • arr.mean() hoc np.mean(arr): Trung bnh cng ca tt c cc phn t trong mng arr.
  • np.median(arr): Tr v gi tr trung v ca mng arr.
