Tổng quan về Pandas trong Python¶

Pandas l mt th vin Python cung cp cc cu trc d liu nhanh, mnh m, linh hot v mang hm . Tn th vin c bt ngun t panel data (bng d liu). Pandas c thit k lm vic d dng v trc quan vi d liu c cu trc (dng bng, a chiu, c tim nng khng ng nht) v d liu chui thi gian.

Vì sao nên dùng pandas ?¶

Pandas ph hp vi nhiu loi d liu khc nhau:

  • D liu dng bng vi cc ct c nhp khng ng nht, nh trong bng SQL hoc bng tnh Excel.
  • D liu chui thi gian theo th t v khng c th t (khng nht thit phi c tn s c nh).
  • D liu ma trn ty (c nhp ng nht hoc khng ng nht) vi nhn hng v ct.
  • Bt k hnh thc khc ca cc b d liu quan st / thng k. D liu thc s khng cn phi c dn nhn vo cu trc d liu pandas.
  • Pandas c xy dng da trn NumPy. Hai cu trc d liu chnh ca pandas l:
    • Series (1 chiu)
    • DataFrame (2 chiu)

Ưu điểm của pandas:¶

  • D dng x l d liu mt mt, c biu th di dng NaN, trong d liu du phy ng cng nh du phy tnh theo ngi dng mong mun: b qua hoc chuyn sang 0
  • Kh nng thay i kch thc: cc ct c th c chn v xa khi DataFrame v cc i tng chiu cao hn
  • Cn chnh d liu t ng v r rng: cc i tng c th c cn chnh r rng vi mt b nhn hoc ngi dng ch cn b qua cc nhn v Series, DataFrame, v.v. t ng cn chnh d liu cho bn trong cc tnh ton
  • Chc nng group by mnh m, linh hot thc hin cc hot ng kt hp phn tch p dng trn cc tp d liu, cho c d liu tng hp v chuyn i
  • D dng chuyn i d liu ri rc (ragged), ch mc khc nhau (differently-indexed) trong cc cu trc d liu khc ca Python v NumPy thnh cc i tng DataFrame
  • Ct lt (slicing) thng minh da trn nhn, lp ch mc a thch (fancy indexing) v tp hp li (subsetting) cc tp d liu ln
  • Gp (merging) v ni (joining) cc tp d liu trc quan
  • Linh hot trong nh hnh li (reshaping) v xoay (pivoting) cc tp d liu
  • Dn nhn phn cp (hierarchical) ca cc trc (c th c nhiu nhn trn mi nh du)
  • Cc cng c IO mnh m ti d liu t cc tp phng (flat file) nh CSV v delimited, tp Excel, c s d liu v lu / ti d liu t nh dng HDF5 cc nhanh
  • Chc nng theo chui thi gian (time series) c th: to phm vi ngy v chuyn i tn s, thng k ca s di chuyn, dch chuyn ngy v tr.
  • Tch hp tt vi cc th vin khc ca python nh SciPy, Matplotlib, Plotly, v.v.
  • Hiu sut tt

1. Series¶

Series([data, index, dtype, name, copy, . . . ])

Series l mng mt chiu ging nh mng Numpy, hay nh mt ct ca mt bng, nhng n bao gm thm mt bng nh label. Series c th c khi to thng qua NumPy, kiu Dict hoc cc d liu v hng bnh thng. Series c nhiu thuc tnh nh index, array, values, dtype, v.v. Bn c th thc hin chuyn i Series sang dng dtype xc nh, to bng copy, tr v dng bool ca mt thnh phn, chuyn Series t DatetimeIndex sang PeriodIndex, v.v

2. DataFrame¶

DataFrame([data, index, columns, dtype, copy])

Dataframe l cu trc d liu c gn nhn hai chiu vi cc ct v hng nh bng tnh (spreadsheet) hoc bng (table). Ging nh Series, DataFrame c th cha bt k loi d liu no. Mt iu quan trng cn lm ni bt l tt c cc ct trong khung d liu l series Pandas. V vy, mt DataFrame l s kt hp ca nhiu Series ng vai tr nh cc ct! DataFrame c s dng rng ri v l mt trong nhng cu trc d liu quan trng nht.
