subplot ( )

In [1]:
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import numpy as np

Chng ta c th s dng phng thc subplot( ) thm nhiu *plots trong mt hnh.

Phng thc subplot( ) c ba i s: nrows, ncols v index. Chng ch ra s lng hng, s ct v s index ca sub-plot.

subplots( )

Khi chng ta mun c nhiu subplots trong hnh. Mt cch thun tin hn l s dng phng thc subplots().

Lu v s khc bit ca cc gi tr trong c hai phng thc: Phng thc ny ly hai i s nrows v ncols lm s lng hng v s ct tng ng. Phng thc ny to ra hai i tng: figure v axes m chng ta lu tr trong cc bin fig v ax c th c s dng thay i cc thuc tnh mc figure v axes tng ng. Lu rng cc tn bin ny c chn ty .
