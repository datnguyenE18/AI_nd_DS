Đạo hàm của Vector¶

Cho d l Vector, ma trn,... hay g i chng na th o hm lun lun lin quan n s thay i

V d $y = x^2$

$=> y' = 2x$

Khi $x$ thay i 1 th $y$ thay i $2x$, $x$ thay i bao nhiu th $y$ thay i by nhiu. C ngha y th hin s nh hng ca $x$ ln $y$

Bnh thng ch c 1 x nh hng n y. Nhng by gi v d c 1 php nhn v hng:

$y = x_1^2 + x_2^2 + ... + x_n^2 $

Tt c nhng $x$ ny u nh hng ti $y$

Khi ny xut hin Gradient

Gradient¶

o hm tng $x$ mt ri gp li thnh 1 vector:

o hm ring:

Khi bin $y$ b nh hng bi nhiu bin khc th ta dng k hiu $$ th hin cho o hm ring (gi l partial gradient) thay cho $d$, $d$ dng khi khng c ring.

o hm ring trn tng bin nh hng n n ch khng phi ton b

o hm ring ny th hin $x_n$ nh hng n $y$ nh th no

Gradient l tp hp tt c nhng o hm ring ca gi tr c tnh ra vi input u vo. (Gradient l hp tt c nhng Derivative li)

C $n$ bin nh hng n bin $y$ th ta phi tnh tng o hm mt ri ghp li thnh mt vector. Vector y s th hin tt c nhng s nh hng

Ni n Gradient l ta ni n o hm trn Vector hoc mt ma trn - tc l b nh hng bi nhiu bin (Gradient Decent c coi l thut ton - s dng vic cp nht cc Gradient)

Trong khng gian nhiu chiu, Gradient th hin tnh cht ging ht o hm - chnh l dc, s thay i. Trong khng gian nhiu chiu th vic nh hng ti dc, s thay i y do nhiu i tng nh hng n khng ch l duy nht
